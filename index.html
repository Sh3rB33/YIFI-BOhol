YOUTH OF IGLESIA FILIPINA INDEPENDIENTE

Understand Our Logo

YOUTH

Recognized as the strong foundation of the organization, it shall strengthen its solidarity, camaraderie and actively promote its total development to serve God and society.

Pro Deo et Patria

To acknowledge that God as our Divine Providence and we live to be faithful in promoting the Church heritage and serve our country for the glory of his Name.

Iglesia Filipina Independiente

To bear in mind and spirit the historical ministry of our Church in the mission and service of being servants of our God and our country as well.

Vine

The Youth should uphold and unite in the mission of Jesus Christ, carry out his cross and follow his footsteps to be one of his people with the Holy Bible as the strong shield and arm of youth to protect and defend their faith.

Bamboo Cross

A symbol of great love to our Savior.

Knotted Rope

Individual members have their own duties and obligations to protect and develop this organization. Their participation and unity symbolize strength, hopes and power of the Church.

Color Red

It symbolizes the blood of Christ for our Salvation, and the color of bravery as what the servants, martyrs, followers and heroes offered their lives to follow Jesus's footsteps.

Color Blue

Symbolized the honesty, integrity and dignity as a part and member of this Nationalist Church.

Philippine Map

Represent our country, in which we are born, that still needs help and concern for the welfare of our mission and goals.

YIFI Bohol Chapter

Consisting of 13 parishes and 8 mission parishes, the YIFI Bohol population has been constantly progressing as the Youth Organization of the church.
