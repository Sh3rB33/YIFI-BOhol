YOUTH OF IGLESIA FILIPINA INDEPENDIENTE

Consisting of 13 and eight mission parishes, the YIFI Bohol population has been constantly progressing as the church's Youth Organization. With a strong sense of community and a shared mission, the young members of YIFI Bohol have become a driving force in promoting spiritual growth, social responsibility, and leadership development among the church youths. The YIFI Bohol population represents a diverse group of individuals from various backgrounds, but they are united by their faith and commitment to serving God and the country. Through regular gatherings, bible sharing, oikos for Christ, and youth camps, the organization provides a platform for young people to connect, learn, and positively impact their communities.
